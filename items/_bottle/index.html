Bottle

700
Use: Regenerate - Consumes a charge to restore Health and Mana over time. Healing stops if attacked. Bottle can be refilled at your team's fountain. Runes can be stored in the Bottle for later use, and are activated automatically after 2 minutes. If Bottle does not have full charges and is placed on a courier, the courier will move 30% slower.

Hold Control to use Regenerate on an allied hero. Stored runes cannot be shared.
Bottle is shareable.
HEALTH RESTORED: 110
MANA RESTORED: 70
DURATION: 3
An old bottle that survived the ages, the contents placed inside become enchanted.
