Sentry Ward

200
Use: Plants a Sentry Ward, an invisible watcher that grants True Sight, the ability to see invisible enemy units and wards, to any existing allied vision within its range. Lasts 4 minutes.

Hold Control to give one Sentry Ward to an allied hero.
TRUE SIGHT RANGE: 850
DURATION (MINUTES): 4
A form of plant originally grown in the garden of a fearful king.
