Mask of Madness

1800
Active: Berserk - Gives 100 attack speed and 17% movement speed, but causes you to take extra 30% damage. Lasts 12.0 seconds.
Passive: Lifesteal - Heals the attacker for a percentage of attack damage dealt.

Mask of Madness is a Unique Attack Modifier, and does not stack with other Unique Attack Modifiers.
LIFESTEAL: 20%
BERSERK ATTACK SPEED: 100
BERSERK MOVE SPEED: 17%
BERSERK ADDED DAMAGE TAKEN: 30%
BERSERK DURATION: 12
Once this mask is worn, its bearer becomes an uncontrollable aggressive force.
