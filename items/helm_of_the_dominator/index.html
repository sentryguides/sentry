Helm of the Dominator

1800
Active: Dominate - Take control of a non-hero, non-ancient target unit. If a dominated creep's base health is naturally below the health minimum, its base health will be raised to the health minimum.
Passive: Lifesteal - Heals the attacker for a percentage of attack damage dealt.

Helm of the Dominator is a Unique Attack Modifier, and does not stack with other Unique Attack Modifiers.
Cannot dominate more than one unit at a time. If a new unit is dominated, the old one will die.
Selling or upgrading Helm of the Dominator will cause dominated units to die.
