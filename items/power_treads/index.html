Power Treads

1400
Active: Switch Attribute - Changes selected attribute between Strength, Agility and Intelligence.

Power Treads can be built using a Belt of Strength, Band of Elvenskin, or a Robe of the Magi. Flat movement speed bonuses from multiple pairs of boots do not stack, nor does the attack speed bonus from multiple Power Treads.
+ 50 Movement Speed
+ 9 Selected Attribute
+ 25 Attack Speed
A pair of tough-skinned boots that change to meet the demands of the wearer.
