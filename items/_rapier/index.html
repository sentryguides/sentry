Divine Rapier

6200
Drops on death. Becomes unusable if picked up by an ally of its owner until it is returned to its owner. It is immediately usable by anybody if an enemy of the owner picks it up. A dropped Rapier cannot be picked up by a courier.
If Divine Rapier is dropped and picked up by an enemy of its original owner, it cannot be dropped again except by death.
+ 330 Damage
So powerful, it cannot have a single owner.
