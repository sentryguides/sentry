Borrowed Time

When activated, all damage dealt to you will heal instead of harm. Most negative buffs will also be removed. If the ability is not on cooldown, it will automatically activate if your health falls below 400. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
Borrowed Time can be activated while Abaddon is disabled, but not while silenced.
HEALTH THRESHOLD: 400
DURATION: 4 / 5 / 6
SCEPTER DURATION: 5 / 6 / 7
SCEPTER DAMAGE REDIRECT: 50%
SCEPTER REDIRECT RANGE: 900
