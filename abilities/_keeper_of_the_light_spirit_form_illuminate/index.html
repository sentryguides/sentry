Illuminate

Channels light energy, building power the longer it's channeled. Once released, a wave is sent forth that deals damage and gives vision in its path. The longer it is channeled, the more damage is dealt. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter, allowing Illuminate to heal allies and harm enemies during daytime. The heal and damage amounts are the same.
Gives increasing vision over the channeling time.
Damages mechanical units.
