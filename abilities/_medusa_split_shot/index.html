Split Shot

Medusa magically splits her shot into several arrows. These arrows deal a lower percent of her normal damage. The extra targets will not receive other attack effects (such as critical strike) and Unique Attack Modifiers.
Secondary targets will be the closest enemy units excluding the primary target.
If illusions of Medusa are created while Split Shot is active, they will also have this ability.
OUTGOING DAMAGE: 80%
TOTAL TARGETS: 2 / 3 / 4 / 5
RANGE: 700
All who encounter the cursed Gorgon will feel her rage.
