Winter's Curse

Winter Wyvern freezes an enemy in place while striking those nearby with a maddening curse which causes them to attack their frozen ally. Those cursed to attack their ally take 70% less damage.
If the target dies to its allies, Winter Wyvern will receive credit for the kill.
Will only pierce the spell immunity of the primary target, not the target's surrounding allies.
CURSE RADIUS: 400
CURSE DAMAGE REDUCTION: 70%
CURSE DURATION: 2.5 / 3 / 3.5
