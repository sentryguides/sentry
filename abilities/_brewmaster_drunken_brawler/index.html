Drunken Brawler

Gives a chance to avoid attacks and to deal critical damage. Drunken Brawler will always trigger if you have not attacked, or have not been attacked, in the last several seconds. Stacks diminishingly with other sources of Evasion.
The guaranteed critical attack and guaranteed dodge chance are on separate timers.
DODGE CHANCE: 10% / 15% / 20% / 25%
CRIT CHANCE: 10% / 15% / 20% / 25%
CRIT DAMAGE: 200%
CERTAIN TRIGGER TIMER: 16 / 14 / 12 / 10
When Mangix won his title as the Brewmaster of the Order of Oyo, he also claimed his place in the mastery of inebriation.
