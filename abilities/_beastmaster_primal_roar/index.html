Primal Roar

Beastmaster lets loose a deafening roar that stuns, and shoves open, a path to its target. All units in the path of the roar are damaged, while units shoved aside by the roar have their movement and attack speed slowed. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
The stun will hit Spell Immune units. The damage will not.
