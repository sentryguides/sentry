Track

Tracks an enemy hero, granting True Sight of the target, and information on how much gold it is carrying. Allies near the hunted are granted bonus movement speed. If the target dies, Bounty Hunter and nearby heroes collect a bonus bounty of gold.
Bounty Hunter will get the bonus gold if the target dies while Track is active, regardless of how it died.
Track's effect is only visible to allies.
The allies that are receiving the bonus speed from Track are the ones that will receive the bonus gold.
Bonus gold is reliable gold.
