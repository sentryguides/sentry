Duel

Legion Commander and the target enemy hero are forced to attack each other for a short duration. Neither hero can use items or abilities. If either hero dies during the duration, the hero winning the Duel gains permanent bonus damage. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
Duels will end if the units are pushed 2000 units apart.
DURATION: 4 / 4.75 / 5.5
BONUS DAMAGE: 10 / 14 / 18
