Liquid Fire

Jakiro burns his enemies in an area of effect with fire added to his attack, while slowing their attacks. Liquid Fire is a Unique Attack Modifier, and does not stack with other Unique Attack Modifiers.
Works on both units and buildings.

RADIUS: 300 / 300 / 300 / 300
BURN DAMAGE: 12 / 16 / 20 / 24
DURATION: 5 / 5 / 5 / 5
