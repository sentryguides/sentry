Nether Swap

Instantaneously swaps positions with a target Hero, friend or enemy. Nether Swap interrupts channeling abilities on the target. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
Works on Spell Immune units.
Trees near Vengeful Spirit and the target will be destroyed when the spell is cast.
Channeling spells are interrupted by Nether Swap.
