Reaper's Scythe

Stuns the target enemy hero, then deals damage based on how much life it is missing. Heroes killed by Reaper's Scythe will have 30 seconds added to their respawn timer. Any kill under this effect is credited to Necrophos. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
Damage is dealt at the end of the stun.
The stun goes through spell immunity, but not the damage.
