Viper Strike

Viper slows the targeted enemy unit's movement and attack speed while also dealing poison damage over time. The slowing effect reduces over the duration of the poison. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
Slow works on spell immune units.
Units afflicted by Viper Strike can be denied when their HP drops below 25%.
