Chaos Strike

Each of Chaos Knight's attacks has a chance to deal bonus damage and reduce the target's armor by 5 for 8 seconds.
The armor reduction debuff is applied before the damage occurs, similar to Desolator.
Illusions can apply the armor reduction debuff, however it does not stack.
CRITICAL CHANCE: 10% / 10% / 10% / 10%
CRITICAL DAMAGE: 125% / 175% / 225% / 275%
ARMOR REDUCTION: 5
REDUCTION DURATION: 8
Chaos Knight's unwieldy power bludgeons those who stand before him, crushing the thickest of armors.
