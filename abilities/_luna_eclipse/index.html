Eclipse

Showers random nearby enemies with strikes from Luna's current level of Lucent Beam. These beams do not stun their targets, and there is a maximum number of times that a single target can be struck. Also turns day into night for 10 seconds. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
Eclipse doesn't work if Luna doesn't have any points in Lucent Beam.
Beams will stop if Luna is killed.
Eclipse Lucent Beams do not stun.
Will not hit invisible units.
There is a .6 second gap between beams.
