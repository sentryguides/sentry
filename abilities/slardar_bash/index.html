Bash

Grants a chance that an attack will do bonus damage and stun an enemy. The duration is doubled against creeps.
Stun works on Spell Immune units.
Does not stack with Skull Basher.
CHANCE: 10% / 15% / 20% / 25%
BONUS DAMAGE: 60 / 80 / 100 / 120
DURATION: 1
