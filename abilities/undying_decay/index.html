Decay

Undying steals strength from all enemy heroes in an area, dealing base damage as he claims the enemy's strength for himself. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter, increasing Decay's strength steal.
The strength stolen stacks, but can't cause a hero to have strength below 1.
The strength steal takes effect before the damage is dealt.
Affects invisible units.
