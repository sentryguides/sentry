Impetus

Places an enchantment on each attack while activated, causing it to deal additional damage based on how far away the target is. The farther the target, the greater the damage dealt. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
The damage is capped at 2500 distance.
Distance to target is measured when the projectile hits.
