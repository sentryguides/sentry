Decrepify

A powerful banishing spell that slows a unit and renders it unable to attack or be attacked. Afflicted units take extra magic damage.
Only magical and pure damage can affect a unit under the effects of Decrepify.
ALLY INCREASED MAGIC DAMAGE: 25%
ALLY MOVE SLOW: 25%
ENEMY INCREASED MAGIC DAMAGE: 30% / 40% / 50% / 60%
ENEMY MOVE SLOW: 30% / 40% / 50% / 60%
DURATION: 3.5
