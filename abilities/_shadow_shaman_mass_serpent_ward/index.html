Mass Serpent Ward

Summons 10 serpent wards to attack enemy units and structures. The wards are immune to magic, and have a small area of splash damage that increases per level. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
Serpent Wards deal piercing type splash damage.
Serpent Wards deal 100% damage in a 50/75/85 radius.
Serpent Wards deal 40% damage in a 75/100/110 radius.
Serpent Wards deal 20% damage in a 150/200/220 radius.
