Life Drain

CHANNELED - When cast on an enemy, Pugna drains health from the target enemy unit to heal himself. If Pugna has full HP, and the enemy target is a Hero, Life Drain will restore mana instead. When cast on an ally, Pugna will drain his own health into his ally. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
HP drained depends on the actual damage dealt.
Illusions are destroyed on the first tick of damage.
DRAIN PER SECOND: 150 / 200 / 250
CAST RANGE: 900 / 1050 / 1200
MAX DURATION: 10
SCEPTER DRAIN PER SECOND: 180 / 240 / 300
