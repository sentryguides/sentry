Blade Fury

Causes a bladestorm of destructive force around Juggernaut, rendering him immune to magic and dealing damage to nearby enemy units. Lasts 5 seconds.
DAMAGE: 80 / 105 / 130 / 155
This skill doesn't affect mechanical units.
You can use items during Blade Fury.
Juggernaut can still perform attacks while in Blade Fury. However, only units that are not affected by Blade Fury, such as wards or structures, will take damage from these attacks.
