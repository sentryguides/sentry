Soul Assumption

Visage gathers charges of soul essence each time nearby heroes take more than 110 damage. When the essence is released, it deals base damage as well as damage for each gathered soul charge.
Charges are only gained for damage greater than 2 and less than 3000 that is dealt by a player or Roshan. Self-damage or Soul Assumption damage isn't counted.
Soul Assumption cannot be disjointed.
