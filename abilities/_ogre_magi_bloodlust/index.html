Bloodlust

Incites a frenzy in a friendly unit, increasing its movement speed and attack speed. Can be cast on towers.
When multicast, nearby allies will be selected randomly in an 575 area of effect to receive Bloodlust.
Can affect siege units.
Can be cast on Spell Immune allies.
BONUS MOVE SPEED: 10% / 12% / 14% / 16%
BONUS ATTACK SPEED: 30 / 40 / 50 / 60
DURATION: 30
