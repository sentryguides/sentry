Wall of Replica

Raises a wall of warping light that damages and creates replicas of any enemy hero who crosses it. Enemy replicas serve at the Dark Seer's will. Replicas last until they are destroyed, or until the wall's duration ends. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
Doesn't create illusions from other illusions, or Meepo clones.
Creates a wall 1000 units long, perpendicular to the line between Dark Seer and the target point.
