Berserker's Rage

While active, Troll Warlord swaps from using ranged to melee attacks. Melee attacks have a chance to bash, dealing bonus damage and stunning the target. Berserker's Rage also grants additional health, movement speed, armor, and increased attack speed. Whirling Axes has different functionality while Berserker's Rage is active.
The bash stun goes through spell immunity.
Doesn't stack with Skull Basher.
Manta Style's cooldown time will be based on whether Troll Warlord was in his ranged or melee form when the item was used.
BONUS HEALTH: 100
BONUS MOVE SPEED: 10 / 20 / 30 / 40
BONUS ARMOR: 3
MELEE BASE ATTACK TIME: 1.55
BASH CHANCE: 10%
BASH STUN DURATION: 0.8 / 1.2 / 1.6 / 2
BASH BONUS DAMAGE: 20 / 30 / 40 / 50
Like his anger, Troll Warlord's supply of axes is infinite.
