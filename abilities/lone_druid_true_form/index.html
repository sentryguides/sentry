True Form

Lone Druid learns to morph himself into a raging bear, losing his ranged advantage and some base movement speed, but gaining melee power as well as the Battle Cry ability. He can morph freely between druid and bear form. Upgradable with Aghanim's Scepter.
You can dodge stuns during the 2 second duration of transforming.
Manta Style's cooldown time will be based on whether Lone Druid was in his ranged or melee form when the item was used.
