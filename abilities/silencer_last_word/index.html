Last Word

Enchants a target, causing them to be damaged, silenced, and their movement speed slowed if they cast a spell or if the enchantment timer expires.
Channeling abilities will trigger this effect once the caster finishes its channel.
DAMAGE: 150 / 200 / 250 / 300
ENEMY CAST TIMER: 4
MOVE SLOW: 14% / 16% / 18% / 20%
SILENCE/SLOW DURATION: 3 / 4 / 5 / 6
