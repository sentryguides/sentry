Song of the Siren

All enemies in range of the Naga Siren are put into a magical stasis where they cannot act or be attacked. Using Song of the Siren again will end the duration early. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
Units affected by Song of the Siren are invulnerable and cannot take damage from any source.
Doesn't affect Spell Immune units.
The area of the song follows Naga, so if she moves far enough away from affected units, they will wake up.
