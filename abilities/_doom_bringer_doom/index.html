Doom

Inflicts a curse that prevents an enemy Hero from casting spells or using items, while taking damage over time. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
If a magic shield is present, such as the barrier from Pipe of Insight, Doom will damage the barrier first before moving on to the Hero.
DURATION: 15
DAMAGE PER SECOND: 20 / 35 / 50
SCEPTER DURATION: 16 / 16 / 16
SCEPTER DAMAGE PER SECOND: 40 / 60 / 80
