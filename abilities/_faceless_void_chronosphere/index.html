Chronosphere

Creates a blister in spacetime, trapping all units caught in its sphere of influence and causes you to move very quickly inside it. Only Faceless Void and any units he controls are unaffected. Invisible units in the sphere will be revealed. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter.
Pauses all units and buildings, friendly or enemy, except those owned by Faceless Void.
Faceless Void is never affected by Chronospheres with any owner.
