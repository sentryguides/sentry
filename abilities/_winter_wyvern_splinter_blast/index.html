Splinter Blast

Launches a ball of brittle ice toward an enemy. The ice shatters on impact, leaving the primary target completely unaffected, while hurling damaging splinters into nearby enemies in a 500 radius. Enemies struck by these splinters are slowed by 25%.
DAMAGE: 100 / 180 / 260 / 340
The primary projectile moves at 500 movespeed, or reaches the target in 1 second, whichever is faster up to 4000 speed.
Neither the primary or secondary projectiles can be disjointed.
SHATTER RADIUS: 500
MOVEMENT SLOW: 25%
