Earthbind

Tosses a net at the target point, pinning down all enemy units. Earthbind prevents invisibility, blink, and interrupts channeling.
While preventing units from going invisible, Earthbind won't hit units that are already invisible.
Does not work on Spell Immune units or Roshan.
