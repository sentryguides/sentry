Rolling Boulder

Earth Spirit gathers himself into a boulder and, after a 0.6s delay, rolls toward the target location, damaging enemy units. He will stop if he collides with an enemy hero or is stunned. If he rolls over a Stone Remnant, he will travel further and faster, and enemies hit by the boulder will have their movement speed slowed.
Rolling over a Stone Remnant will destroy the Remnant.
The boulder has a radius of 150.
