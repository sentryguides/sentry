Blinding Light

A blinding light flashes over the targeted area, knocking back and blinding the units in the area, causing them to miss attacks.
Causes knockback of 250 range over 0.4 seconds, centered on the middle of the target area. Upon landing, units will destroy trees in a 150 radius.
Does not interrupt channeling.
