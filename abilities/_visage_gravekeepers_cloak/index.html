Gravekeeper's Cloak

Visage generates a layered barrier that protects him from physical and magical attacks. If he receives damage from a player, one layer is removed, and takes time to recover.
Each time Visage receives damage greater than 2 that was not self-inflicted and was from a player owned source, a layer is removed.
ARMOR PER LAYER: 1 / 2 / 4 / 5
RESIST PER LAYER: 3% / 6% / 12% / 16%
MAX LAYERS: 4
LAYER RECOVERY TIME: 4
Visage's tough scales share equal properties of ghost and stone, making it nearly impervious to blade and magical assault.
