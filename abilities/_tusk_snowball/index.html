Snowball

Tusk begins rolling into a snowball. Allies within a 400 radius can also be added to the snowball by right-clicking on them, even while the snowball is moving. Once launched, any enemies caught in the snowball's path will be stunned and take damage. Each allied Hero in the snowball will add to its damage.
The snowball destroys trees.
The snowball travels at 675 movement speed.
Doesn't carry illusions, but will carry Meepo clones and other units that Tusk controls.
Each Meepo clone added to the snowball will add its own bonus damage and speed.
Allies can click on a snowball to add themselves to it.
BASE DAMAGE: 80 / 120 / 160 / 200
BONUS DAMAGE PER ALLY: 20 / 30 / 40 / 50
STUN DURATION: 0.5 / 0.75 / 1 / 1.25
GATHER RADIUS: 100
LAUNCH TIME: 3
