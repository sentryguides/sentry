Holy Persuasion

Chen takes control of an enemy or neutral creep. If the persuaded creep's base health is naturally below the health minimum, its base health will be raised to the health minimum. Upgradable by Aghanim's Scepter, allowing Chen to persuade Ancient Creeps. The number of Ancient Creeps that can be persuaded is based on the level of Hand of God.
You may only cast this ability on Ancient Creeps with Aghanim's Scepter.
When persuading a new creep above your max unit count, the oldest persuaded unit will die.
The health of creeps that are currently persuaded will not be increased further if more levels are added to the skill.
MAX UNITS: 1 / 2 / 3 / 4
HEALTH MINIMUM: 700 / 800 / 900 / 1000
