Fury Swipes

Ursa's claws dig deeper wounds in the enemy, causing consecutive attacks to the same enemy to deal more damage. If the same target is not attacked after 15 seconds, the bonus damage is lost.
RESET TIME: 15
RESET TIME (ROSHAN): 6
DAMAGE PER ATTACK: 15 / 20 / 25 / 30
In nature, the wounded rarely survive the attacks of hungry predators.
